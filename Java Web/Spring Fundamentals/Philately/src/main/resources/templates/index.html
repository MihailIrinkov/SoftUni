Welcome to Philately App

Philately is the collection and study of postage stamps and related postal items.
It is a hobby enjoyed by people of all ages around the world, and it
can be both a leisure activity and an academic pursuit.

Philately began shortly after the introduction of the first postage stamp, the Penny Black,
in 1840 in Great Britain. As postal systems expanded and evolved, so did the hobby of collecting stamps.
